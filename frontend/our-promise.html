Our Promise

We are committed to bringing you the highest standard of care, transparency, and love when uniting you with your new best friend.

No Puppy Mills. Ever.

Our zero-tolerance policy against puppy mills and substandard operations ensures that every puppy comes from a vetted, responsible, and caring environment. We manually inspect and review all of our breeding partners.

Safe & Supervised Travel

We orchestrate specialized travel logistics tailored to each individual puppy's needs. Whether flying with a flight nanny or safely utilizing our secure pet transit ground network, your puppy arrives safe and sound.

24/7 Concierge Support

Our relationship doesn't end once you take your puppy home. From expert nutritional guidance to behavior coaching, our team and Scientific Advisory Board are always accessible via WhatsApp for anything you need.
