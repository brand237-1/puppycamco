Breeder Standards

Less than 10% of breeders who apply are accepted into our exclusive network. We enforce the most rigorous checks in the industry.

Comprehensive Vetting

Before a breeder is invited to join Puppy Cam Co, they must undergo an extensive screening process. This includes an interview, an evaluation of their breeding facilities, and verification of their veterinary records and USDA status if applicable.

Veterinary Care

Our breeders are required to provide comprehensive, documented, timely, and species-appropriate preventative care, vaccinations, and parasite control, ensuring their breeding parents and puppies are healthy.

Safe Environments

We require all housing facilities to be spacious, clean, well-lit, and well-ventilated. Dogs must have daily access to exercise and space to play safely.

Socialization

Puppies are raised in loving environments where they get accustomed to various sounds, environments, and people, ensuring they graduate as well-rounded companions.
