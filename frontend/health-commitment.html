10-Year Health Commitment

We don't just promise healthy puppies; we stand by them for a decade.

Our Promise to You

We are proud to offer an industry-leading 10-Year Health Commitment for every puppy adopted through the Puppy Cam Co network. Our dedication to the health and well-being of our puppies is rooted in the rigorous vetting of our breeders and the guidance of our Scientific Advisory Board.

This commitment means that if your puppy is diagnosed with a covered genetic or hereditary condition within their first 10 years of life, we are here to support you and your furry family member.

What Is Covered

  • Severe, life-altering genetic defects
  • Hip and elbow dysplasia
  • Congenital heart defects
  • Hereditary eye disorders

Required Steps

  • A wellness exam by a licensed vet within 3 days of arrival
  • Keeping vaccinations and deworming up to date
  • Feeding your puppy a high-quality, species-appropriate diet
  • Providing regular exercise and maintaining a healthy weight
